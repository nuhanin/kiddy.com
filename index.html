Creative Clash

A game of satire for 2-5 players

Objective: Lead your creative agency to victory in a humorous battle for the biggest ego around!

Winning Condition: Be the first to achieve your Principal's Ego goal on your turn to win!

Components

  • Principal Cards (gray deck)
  • Nine To Five Cards (black deck)
  • Employees
  • Skills
  • Projects
  • Stuff
  • Events
  • Persons
  • Experts
  • Player Boards
  • Wooden Ego Tokens
  • Wooden Coin Tokens
  • Active Player Token

Setup

  1. Give 1 player board, 1 Ego token, and 1 Coin token to each player.
  2. Shuffle the Principal cards and deal one card to each player. Put the remaining Principal cards aside for the rest of the game.
  3. Remove the Expert cards from the Nine to Five deck if you wish.
  4. Shuffle the Nine To Five deck and place it in the middle of the table.
  5. Players must discard their hand down to 5 cards.

Turn Order

  1. Collect Income
  2. Draw 2 Cards
